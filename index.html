Dear Riara Students, Exciting adventures await you this year with two incredible trips on offer: one to Dubai and the other to Arusha. In Dubai, you'll immerse yourselves in the glitz and glamour of this vibrant city, exploring iconic landmarks such as the Burj Khalifa, the Palm Jumeirah, and the Dubai Mall. You'll also have the opportunity to experience the rich culture and heritage of the region by visiting the historic Al Fahidi Fort and indulging in a traditional Arabian dinner under the starlit desert sky. The Dubai trip will cost approximately $1400 per student, inclusive of accommodation, transportation, and guided tours. Alternatively, venture into the heart of East Africa with our Arusha trip, where you'll be captivated by the breathtaking landscapes and wildlife of Tanzania. Embark on thrilling safaris in the Serengeti National Park and Ngorongoro Crater, where you can spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and more in their natural habitats. Additionally, visit the vibrant markets of Arusha and immerse yourselves in the local Maasai culture. The Arusha trip will cost approximately $250 per student, covering accommodation, transportation, park fees, and guided excursions. Don't miss out on these unforgettable journeys filled with cultural immersion, adventure, and lifelong memories. Secure your spot now and get ready for the experience of a lifetime!
